Why v-Reach.!

Our Motto is simple: "Provide Resources and Oppurtunities to reach back to our Tamil roots."
Tamil (தமிழ் tamiḻ) is a classical language and one of the major languages of the Dravidian language family. Spoken predominantly by Tamils in India, Sri Lanka, Malaysia, and Singapore, it has smaller communities of speakers in many other countries. It is the eighteenth most spoken language, with over 87 million speakers worldwide.Tamil is considered the oldest living language in the world, with a history spanning more than 5,000 years. Tamil is one of the few living classical languages, meaning that it meets three criteria: its origins are ancient; it has an independent tradition; and it possesses a considerable body of ancient literature. Hence, It is a Honour and also a responsibility to reach out and to strengthen our roots with the tamil language, culture and heritage. There are several tamilians living away from Tamil homeland. And new generation of Tamil kids are born in foreign countries. Many have passion to stay connected with Tamil heritage. For that essence "vReach" provides traditional Tamil products, pointers to Tamil teaching centers, notify about tamil events, all with an intent to make you feel connected to the Tamil Roots.
